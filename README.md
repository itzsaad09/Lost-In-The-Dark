# Lost In The Dark

**Lost In The Dark** is a first-person survival horror game built with Unity. Immerse yourself in a chilling atmosphere where exploration, stealth, and resource management are key to survival.

## üìñ Overview

In "Lost In The Dark", players navigate through eerie environments, solving puzzles and uncovering secrets while avoiding the dangers that lurk in the shadows. With a focus on atmosphere and tension, every step could be your last.

## ‚ú® Features

- **Immersive First-Person Experience:** Realistic FPS controller with smooth movement and interaction.
- **Interactive Environment:** Open doors, search drawers, flip light switches, and inspect objects to find clues.
- **Survival Mechanics:** Manage limited resources like **Lantern Fuel** and **Camera Batteries** to keep the darkness at bay.
- **Stealth & Hiding:** Use the environment to your advantage. Hide in closets or under furniture to evade enemies.
- **Inventory System:** Collect and manage items, keys, and tools necessary for progression.
- **Photography System:** Capture evidence or document your journey with an in-game camera and photo album.
- **Dynamic Horror:** random events like flickering lights and stalking enemies (Zombies) keep the tension high.
- **Multi-Level Progression:** Explore distinct levels (Level 1, Level 2, Level 3) with increasing difficulty and narrative depth.
